Our Mission:

Laterem Labs is a multifaceted dynamic creative agency, we turn your dreams into reality. We offer a wide range of services from technical to artistic. Laterem has developed interactive art installations, real-time graphics, 360 video experiences, AR (Augmented Reality) mobile apps, interactive WebGL web experiences, Alternative Reality Games, led design of an underwater LED installation, contributed to open source hardware and software development, as well as working for an award winning fasion designer creating 3D models for CNC machining.

Prototyping

Due to the interdisciplinary approach at Laterem we are able to offer services ranging from electronic CAD design/prototyping, Software/firmwire development, 3D modelling, branding and social media automation. We are able to offer 3D renders, sound design, film editing, real-time graphics/motion graphics, bespoke art intallations as well as electrical engineering/archetechturual consultancy on large scale artistic installations. We have drafted proposals and designed a number of large multi-media light sculpture installations for art collectives and professional artists, they have the dream; we turn it into reality.

Manufacture

Our product designs can be made with manufacture in mind. We create forms that manifest with the use of conventional techniques such as Fibre Laser cutting (2D sheet metal), Deposition and Selective Laser Sintering (3D printing), 2.5 and 3 axis milling machines (CNC Milling).

Research and development

Laterem Labs share a deep interest in sustainable product and archetectural development, from investigating Flax as an alternative to carbon fibre, to exploring biological and ecologically manufactured resins, to re-using and recycling materials to faciltate construction.

Capabilites

CAD/CAM, 3D modelling, Composite design, Mould manufacturing, Electronic engineering, Software archetechture, Games design, Realtime Graphics, Interface design, Social Media Automation, Branding and Marketing.

Facilities

Laterem Labs can instantly prototype products with access to FDM and SLA 3D printers, CO2 laser cutters and a 2.5-axis CNC milling machine. For critial tolerences in metal fabrication we lever SLS printing and Amada 6Kw Fibre Laser cutters. We are able to offer a turnkey solution to entrepreneurs, business and brand owners. From concept through to manufacture we can help materialise your dreams.
